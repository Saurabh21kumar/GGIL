GAIL Global IFSC Limited (GGIL) is a Private Limited Company and a wholly-owned subsidiary of GAIL (India) Limited, incorporated on April 7, 2025.

GAIL Global IFSC Limited (GGIL) was strategically established in GIFT City to operate as a Global/Regional Corporate Treasury Centre, aligning with the framework set by the International Financial Services Centres Authority (IFSCA).

Our journey to becoming a fully operational entity includes several key milestones, beginning on April 25, 2025, when we applied for registration as a Finance Company under IFSCA, adhering to the IFSCA (Finance Company) Regulations, 2021, to undertake Global/Regional Corporate Treasury Centre activities.
